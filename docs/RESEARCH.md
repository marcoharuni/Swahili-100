# Research Log

Every decision, experiment, result, and failure is documented here.

## Entry Format

Each entry follows this format:

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — Title

**Context:** Why this experiment/decision was needed.
**Action:** What was done.
**Result:** What happened.
**Decision:** What was decided based on the result.
**Cost:** Compute cost (if any).
```
